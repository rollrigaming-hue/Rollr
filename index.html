01 · THE RETENTION GAP

iGaming is won after the first deposit. Not before.

Every operator has an acquisition strategy.
Few have a retention strategy built around community and long-term engagement.
Without a sense of belonging, loyalty fades and every new acquisition becomes more expensive.

Of newly acquired players become inactive within the first 30 days.

Short Player Lifecycles

No Community

02 · THE RETENTION ADVANTAGE

Retention is the new competitive edge.

The operators that outperform don't just acquire more players.
They create communities, build lasting relationships and give players a reason to come back.
Retention becomes a competitive advantage that compounds over time.

Higher Player Retention
Players stay engaged when they feel part of something beyond the platform.
Higher Player Lifetime Value
Long-term relationships create more opportunities than short-term campaigns.
Brand-Owned Communities
Build loyalty that belongs to your brand—not to the next promotion or creator.
